WHAT WE DO?

We help successful salaried Indian professionals worldwide grow their personal wealth to over 40 times their annual expenses by investing smartly, so that they can build lasting wealth for themselves and their families and be financially free to do what matters most to them; without having to spend too much time tracking their investments or dealing with product sellers.

HOW WE DO IT?

Through a well-researched process of life planning and comprehensive financial planning, We help our clients navigate through their own beliefs and relationships with money and set for themselves inspiring goals. We then chart out a detailed plan of action to get to these goals. We also help our clients to implement the plan and handhold them through the choppy journey of risk management, debt management, investing and succession planning till they reach their goals.

WHY DOES IT WORK?

Money is less about investing and more about behaviors. By focusing on the goals, beliefs, habits and all things controllable, our clients get into the investing world with open eyes and an open mind. With periodic interventions our clients know what is happening with their money and why it is happening. It gives them the courage to stick to plans - which eventually works.

What to expect from us?

  • Onboarding Meeting: A 60-minute meeting where we get clear on your financial goals and values, what your current financial picture looks like, and what current limitations or hurdles you are facing.
  • Plan Delivery Meeting: A 90-Minute meeting to deliver your financial planning recommendations, actions items, and prioritized “to-do” list. You’ll receive a copy of your financial plan a few days before this meeting for review.
  • 30 Day Check-In: A 60-Minute call within one month of recommendation delivery to review progress.
  • 6 Month Reviews: A 60-Minute review meeting every 6 months to measure progress, make adjustments, identify new action steps and address life changes.
  • Ongoing Access: E-mails and calls for accountability and to address changes along the way.
  • Partnered with a Financial Planner: Professional, friendly and encouraging advice from a Financial planner, who will always act in your best interest.
  • Comprehensive Financial Plan: 10-15-page financial plan complete with an itemized action checklist, an overview of your most important goals, a net worth statement, a cash flow projection, retirement projections, and recommendations in the below areas that apply.
  • Investment Advice: Before we invest funds on your behalf, we will review your goals, risk tolerance, time horizon, tax considerations, income needs, and any unique needs that you might have.
